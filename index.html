Step 1 of 6

Your board

This is the board. It has two sides: your's, and your opponents. You will play your minion cards onthe board and will be trying to keep it full as you and your opponent begin to trade*.

Trading: The act of using your minions of greater power to even out the board by destroying your opponents minions.

Step 2 of 6

Mana

Your mana will allow you to play your minions. Each minion has a unique mana cost. You will need to you your mana pool wisely to win a game.

You begin the game with 1 mana and will gain 1 every turn until you have 10.

Step 3 of 6

Classes

Druid

The druid is one with nature. Using elves, druids and treants he will easily control the board. For 2 mana, he can get 1 attack and 1 armor.
Close

Hunter

The hunter uses his beasts to hunt down his opponent. For 2 mana he can deal 2 damage to his enemy.
Close

Mage

The mage uses powerful spells to tear her enemies apart. She can deal 1 damage to any character for the cost of 2 mana.
Close

Paladin

The paladin uses weapons and knights to fight. For 2 mana he can summon a 1/1 recruit.

Close

Priest

The priest uses the opponents cards against them and uses healing to his advantage. For 2 mana, he can heal 2 health from a damaged character.
Close

Rogue

Rogues use weapons as well as stealth to defeat their enemies. For 2 mana, she can equip a 1 damage, 2 durability weapon.
Close

Shaman

The shaman has the elements at his side. He can cast powerful spells and play powerful minions at the cost odf locking away some of his mana on the next turn. Each turn he can summon a random totem for 2 mana.
Close

Warlock

The warlock uses demons to do his bidding. at the cost of 2 health and 2 mana he can draw a card per turn.
Close

Warrior

The warrior class uses weapons. He can gain 2 armor each turn for the cost of 2 mana.

Step 6 of 6

The ranked ladder

The ranked ladder is your ultimate challenge. There are 26 ranks. You shall begin at rank 25 and gain 1 star* per win. Every times you reach 3 stars you will go up a rank. However, every loss will make you lose a star. The final rank is Legend and only a select amount of players can reach it.

After 3 consecutive wins, you will be on a win streak netting you an extra star per win.
